### EDA on Food Delivery Company (Python)

**Description:**  
Performed EDA on a food delivery dataset using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) to analyze restaurant prices, ratings, cuisines, and delivery times across major Indian cities. Derived insights on top cuisines, best-rated areas, and delivery patterns to support data-driven business decisions.

### Key Insights

- Most restaurant prices fall between **₹100–₹500**  
- **Top Restaurants:** La Pino’z Pizza, NIC Natural Ice Creams, Baskin Robbins  
- **Popular Cuisines:** Indian, Chinese, North Indian  
- **Combination Cuisine Trend:** Indian + Chinese dishes are highly preferred  
- **Highest Median Ratings:** South Indian food  
- **Top Rated Area:** Girish Park  
- **Most Rated Restaurant:** SHAH Ghouse Cafe and Restaurant


**Tools:** Python | Pandas | NumPy | Matplotlib | Seaborn
